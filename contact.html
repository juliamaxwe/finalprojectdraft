Frequently Asked Questions

Can you ship me cookies?

We can't ship, unfortunately, because of Ohio's Cottage Laws regarding home bakeries.

What is the allergy information for the cookies?

Our cookies contain gluten, nuts, eggs, and dairy products. We can make gluten free cookies for a slightly higher price, but we are not a gluten free or nut free kitchen.

Will you deliver cookies if I live nearby?

Yes!

How do I get my cookies?

You can come pick them up, or we will deliver them if you live in the Mansfield area!
